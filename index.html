Privacy Policy - Bunker Stem

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Bunker Stem ("the app") is an offline-first evidence workspace for bunker delivery. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

Summary

Bunker Stem does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or operational data. The app has no account system and no server. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. We do not operate a backend and we do not receive any data from the app.

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not transmit your stems, BDN figures, photos, or evidence packs anywhere.

Data stored on your device

All operational data you create, including stems, quantity figures, notes, photos, and generated evidence-pack PDFs, is stored locally on your device only. You control it. It is removed when you delete the data in-app or uninstall the app.

Device permissions

  • Camera is used only on-device to photograph the BDN for optical character recognition (OCR) and to scan sample-bottle, barge QR, and barcodes. Images are processed on the device and are not uploaded.
  • Location is optional and used only to attach a local geotag to photo exhibits when you allow it. Location data is not uploaded or shared.
  • Biometric, such as fingerprint or face unlock, is used only to locally unlock the sign/export action. Biometric data never leaves the device and is handled by Android.
  • Notifications are optional and used only for local reminder alerts before planned bunkering operations.

On-device OCR and barcode recognition use Google ML Kit, which runs locally and does not send images to Google for these features.

Purchases

Export access is sold as an in-app purchase or subscription processed by Google Play Billing. Payment is handled by Google; we do not receive or store your payment details. See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google processes payments.

Children

The app is intended for maritime professionals and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change.
